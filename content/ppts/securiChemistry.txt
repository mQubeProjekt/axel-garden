The interface exchanges only iChemistry-specific identifiers. SAP material numbers are never transmitted outside the SAP landscape.

SAP maintains an internal mapping between the SAP Material Number and the corresponding iChemistry identifier. Only the iChemistry identifier is sent to the external service.

The authentication service returns a temporary access token that is valid only for accessing documents within the iChemistry platform. The token cannot be used to authenticate against SAP systems or to access SAP resources.

No personal data, customer information, financial information, pricing data, or other confidential SAP business data is exchanged via the interface.

The exchanged business data is therefore considered low sensitivity. The only security-sensitive information is the temporary authentication token, which is protected by HTTPS/TLS during transport and is limited to the iChemistry service.
